Say Hello To the Bad Guy

They say I'm the bad guy!

When I started this project, I could not think of an idea or title! Suddenly, I found myself typing the lyrics to a song by American Rapper, Jay-Z.

Say Hello by Jay-Z, born Shawn Carter, was track number 11 on the artist's 10th No. 1 album, American Gangster, inspired by the 2007 film of the same name, starring Denzel Washington and Russell Crowe.
